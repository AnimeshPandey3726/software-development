QA Strategy Summary

Quality Risks & Dependencies

  • Risk: Lack of multi-factor authentication for critical operations. Impact: Increased risk of unauthorized access. Mitigation: Implement multi-factor authentication.
  • Risk: Insufficient encryption for sensitive data in transit. Impact: Increased risk of data breaches. Mitigation: Enhance encryption using TLS.
  • Risk: No rate limiting implemented for APIs. Impact: Increased risk of brute force attacks. Mitigation: Implement rate limiting.
  • Risk: Audit logs are not sufficiently detailed. Impact: Difficulty in forensic analysis. Mitigation: Improve audit logs with detailed information.
  • Risk: No data masking implemented for sensitive information. Impact: Increased risk of data exposure. Mitigation: Implement data masking.
