Engineering Summary

The Hospitable Management System is designed to streamline hotel operations, enhance guest experiences, and improve overall efficiency. It integrates various modules to manage reservations, guest services, housekeeping, and financials.

Backend Architecture Overview

The backend architecture follows a microservices pattern with REST APIs for integration. Each microservice is responsible for a specific business function, such as reservation management, guest services, housekeeping, financial management, and feedback collection.

Engineering Risks

Data loss due to database failure

Impact: Loss of guest and reservation data. Mitigation: Implement regular backups and disaster recovery plans.

Security breach due to weak authentication

Impact: Unauthorized access to sensitive data. Mitigation: Implement strong authentication mechanisms and regular security audits.

Performance degradation due to high traffic

Impact: Slow response times and poor user experience. Mitigation: Implement load balancing and auto-scaling.

Non-compliance with GDPR and HIPAA

Impact: Legal penalties and loss of customer trust. Mitigation: Ensure compliance with data protection regulations and conduct regular audits.

Integration failures with external systems

Impact: Disruption of hotel operations. Mitigation: Implement robust error handling and monitoring for external integrations.
