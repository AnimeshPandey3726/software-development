Key Risks

  • Risk: Inadequate security measures. Impact: Potential data breaches. Mitigation: Implement MFA and enhance security controls.

  • Risk: Non-compliance with regulations. Impact: Legal and financial penalties. Mitigation: Ensure compliance with GDPR and CCPA.
