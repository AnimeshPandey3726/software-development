Deployment Readiness Summary

The Loan Management System (LMS) is ready for deployment with conditions. The system demonstrates a well-structured microservices architecture with clear separation of concerns and adherence to REST principles. However, there are areas for improvement in security, performance, and maintainability. The deployment strategy involves a phased rollout approach, starting with a small subset of users and gradually expanding to a larger user base. Continuous monitoring and feedback loops will ensure a smooth transition.

Deployment Strategy

The deployment strategy for the Loan Management System (LMS) involves a phased rollout approach. Initially, a small subset of users will be onboarded to the new system to validate its functionality and performance. Following successful validation, the system will be gradually rolled out to a larger user base, with continuous monitoring and feedback loops to ensure a smooth transition.
