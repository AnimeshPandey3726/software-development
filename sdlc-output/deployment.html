Deployment Readiness Summary

The Loan Management System is operationally ready for deployment with a medium risk profile. The system has passed all critical tests and is aligned with business requirements. However, some conditions need to be met before final approval.

Deployment Strategy

The deployment strategy for the Loan Management System will utilize a Blue-Green Deployment approach. This method ensures minimal downtime and risk by deploying the new version alongside the current production version. Once validated, traffic will be routed to the new version, allowing for a seamless transition.
