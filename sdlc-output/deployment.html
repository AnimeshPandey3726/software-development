Deployment Readiness Summary

The Hospitable Management System (HMS) is prepared for a phased rollout with conditions. The system has demonstrated robust performance, security, and functionality. However, there are areas for improvement in maintainability and delivery readiness. The deployment strategy involves a controlled rollout to a subset of users, followed by gradual expansion based on feedback and monitoring.

Deployment Strategy

The deployment strategy for the Hospitable Management System (HMS) involves a phased rollout approach. Initially, a small subset of users will be onboarded to the new system, allowing for monitoring and quick issue resolution. Following successful initial deployment, the system will be gradually rolled out to a larger user base, with continuous monitoring and feedback collection. This approach ensures a controlled and manageable deployment process, minimizing potential disruptions and allowing for iterative improvements based on user feedback.
