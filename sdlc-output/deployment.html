The deployment posture for the Hospitable Management System is positive with some conditions. The system is operationally ready with a medium risk profile. Go-live confidence is high with the implementation of necessary remediations.

Deployment Strategy

The deployment strategy for the Hospitable Management System will utilize a Blue-Green Deployment approach. This strategy involves deploying the new version of the application alongside the existing version, allowing for a seamless switch to the new version once it is validated. This approach minimizes downtime and reduces the risk of deployment failures.
