Architecture Overview

The HMS utilizes a Microservices architecture to promote modularity, scalability, and resilience. Each business function is encapsulated within a separate service, enabling independent deployment and scaling. This approach allows for rapid iteration and adaptation to changing business needs while maintaining a robust and secure platform. Security is a primary concern, with authentication and authorization implemented at each service boundary. Scalability is achieved through horizontal scaling of services and leveraging cloud-based infrastructure.
