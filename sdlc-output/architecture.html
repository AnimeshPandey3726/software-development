Loan Management System

Streamlining loan processes for efficiency and customer satisfaction

Business Summary

The Loan Management System is designed to streamline the process of loan application, approval, disbursement, and repayment. It aims to provide a user-friendly interface for customers, efficient workflow for loan officers, and robust reporting for management.

Key Stakeholders / Actors

  • Customers
  • Loan Officers
  • Underwriters
  • Compliance Officers
  • IT Support
  • Management

Functional Requirements

  • Customer registration and login
  • Loan application submission
  • Document upload
  • Loan approval workflow
  • Loan disbursement
  • Repayment scheduling and tracking
  • Notifications and alerts
  • Reporting and analytics

Non-Functional Requirements

  • High availability
  • Scalability
  • Security
  • Performance
  • Compliance with financial regulations

Technology Stack

Frontend: React

Backend: Node.js

Database: PostgreSQL

Cloud: AWS

CI/CD: Jenkins
