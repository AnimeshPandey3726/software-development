Architecture Overview

The LMS utilizes a microservices architecture to promote modularity, scalability, and resilience. Each business function is implemented as an independent service, communicating via REST APIs and message queues. This approach allows for independent deployment and scaling of individual services, enabling rapid innovation and adaptation to changing business needs.
