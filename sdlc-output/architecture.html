Architecture Overview

The Phoenix Commerce Platform utilizes a microservices architecture to achieve scalability, resilience, and agility. Each microservice is independently deployable and scalable, allowing for rapid iteration and innovation. The platform leverages REST APIs for communication between services and integrates with various external systems to provide a seamless customer experience.
