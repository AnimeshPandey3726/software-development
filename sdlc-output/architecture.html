Architecture Overview

The Phoenix Commerce Platform utilizes a microservices architecture to promote modularity, scalability, and resilience. Each business capability is implemented as an independent service, communicating via REST APIs and message queues. This approach allows for independent deployment and scaling of services, enabling rapid innovation and adaptation to changing business needs. Security is a top priority, with robust authentication, authorization, and data protection mechanisms in place.
