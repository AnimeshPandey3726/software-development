Business Summary

The Loan Management System is designed to streamline the loan application, approval, disbursement, and repayment processes. It aims to enhance operational efficiency, reduce manual errors, and improve customer satisfaction.

Architecture Overview

The Loan Management System follows a microservices architecture pattern. Each microservice is responsible for a specific business capability, allowing for independent development, deployment, and scaling. The system is designed to be scalable, secure, and compliant with relevant regulations.

Core Modules

Loan Application

Handles loan application submission and validation.

Loan Approval

Manages loan approval workflow and decision-making.

Loan Disbursement

Handles loan disbursement process and fund transfer.

Loan Repayment

Manages loan repayment process and payment collection.

Customer Management

Manages customer information and profiles.

Loan Officer Management

Manages loan officer information and assignments.

Underwriter Management

Manages underwriter information and assignments.

Disbursement Officer Management

Manages disbursement officer information and assignments.

System Administration

Manages system configuration, users, and roles.

Domain Model

Customer

Represents a customer applying for a loan.

Loan Application

Represents a loan application submitted by a customer.

Loan

Represents a loan granted to a customer.

Loan Disbursement

Represents the disbursement of loan funds to a customer.

Loan Repayment

Represents a loan repayment made by a customer.

Loan Officer

Represents a loan officer responsible for loan applications.

Underwriter

Represents an underwriter responsible for loan approvals.

Disbursement Officer

Represents a disbursement officer responsible for loan disbursements.

System Administrator

Represents a system administrator responsible for system configuration and management.
