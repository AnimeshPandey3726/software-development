Business Summary

The Hospitable Management System is designed to streamline hotel operations, enhance guest experiences, and improve overall efficiency. It integrates various modules to manage reservations, guest services, housekeeping, and financials.

Architecture Overview

The Hospitable Management System follows a microservices architecture pattern. This design allows for independent deployment and scaling of services, enhancing flexibility and resilience. The system is designed to be highly available, secure, and compliant with industry standards.
