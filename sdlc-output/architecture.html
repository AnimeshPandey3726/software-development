Business Summary

The Hospitable Management System is designed to streamline hotel operations, enhance guest experiences, and optimize resource management. It aims to provide a comprehensive solution for hotel management, including reservations, guest services, housekeeping, and financial management.

Architecture Overview

The Hospitable Management System follows a microservices architecture pattern. Each microservice is responsible for a specific business capability, allowing for independent development, deployment, and scaling. The system is designed to be highly available, secure, and scalable, with a focus on providing a seamless guest experience and efficient hotel operations.
