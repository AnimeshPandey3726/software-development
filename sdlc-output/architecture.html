Architecture Overview

The LMS utilizes a microservices architecture to promote modularity, scalability, and resilience. Each business function is encapsulated within a separate service, communicating via REST APIs. This approach allows for independent deployment and scaling of individual components, facilitating agility and innovation. Security is a paramount concern, with robust authentication and authorization mechanisms implemented throughout the system.
