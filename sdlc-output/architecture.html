Architecture Overview

The LMS utilizes a microservices architecture to promote modularity, scalability, and resilience. Each service is independently deployable and scalable, allowing for rapid iteration and adaptation to changing business needs. Security is a paramount concern, with robust authentication, authorization, and data protection mechanisms implemented throughout the system.
