Loan Management System

Streamlining the loan application, approval, disbursement, and repayment process

Business Summary

The Loan Management System is designed to streamline the process of loan application, approval, disbursement, and repayment. It integrates with external credit bureaus and payment gateways to ensure a seamless user experience.
