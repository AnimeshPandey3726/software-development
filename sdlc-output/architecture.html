Business Summary

The Hospitable Management System is designed to streamline hotel operations, enhance guest experiences, and optimize resource management. It aims to provide a comprehensive platform for managing reservations, guest services, and operational workflows.

Architecture Overview

The Hospitable Management System follows a microservices architecture pattern. This design allows for independent deployment, scalability, and maintenance of each service. The system is designed to be highly available, secure, and compliant with GDPR and HIPAA regulations.
