Loan Management System

The Loan Management System is designed to streamline the loan application, approval, and disbursement processes. It aims to enhance operational efficiency, improve customer experience, and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

Leadership View

Business Objective

To provide a seamless and efficient loan management process for customers and loan officers.

Strategic Value

Enhance customer satisfaction and operational efficiency through automation and streamlined processes.

Technology Direction

Leverage modern technologies such as microservices, cloud computing, and DevOps practices.

Delivery Confidence

High, based on proven technology stack and experienced development team.

Executive Summary

Business Challenge: Manual loan processing is time-consuming and error-prone.
Proposed Solution: Automated Loan Management System.
Architecture Rationale: Microservices architecture for scalability and maintainability.
Expected Outcome: Improved efficiency, reduced errors, and enhanced customer experience.

Business Value

Revenue Impact

Increased loan processing volume and faster disbursement.

Operational Efficiency

Reduced manual effort and faster processing times.

Customer Experience

Seamless and quick loan application process.

Scalability Benefits

Easy scaling of services to handle increased load.

Risk Reduction

Automated compliance checks and risk assessments.

Architecture Overview

The system follows a microservices architecture pattern, allowing for independent deployment and scaling of services.

Functional Requirements

  1. Customer loan application submission
  2. Loan officer review and approval
  3. Underwriter risk assessment
  4. Compliance check
  5. Disbursement of approved loans
  6. Customer notification
  7. Reporting and analytics

Non-Functional Requirements

  • High availability
  • Scalability
  • Security
  • Performance
  • Maintainability

Technology Stack

Frontend: React, TypeScript
Backend: Node.js, Express
Database: PostgreSQL, MongoDB
Cloud: AWS
Security: OAuth2, JWT
DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes
Monitoring: Prometheus, Grafana

Architecture Pattern

Microservices

  • Reporting

Domain Model

Entities: Customer, Loan, Application, Review, Assessment, Compliance, Disbursement, Notification
