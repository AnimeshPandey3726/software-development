Loan Management System

Streamlining loan application, approval, and management processes

Business Summary

The Loan Management System is designed to streamline the loan application, approval, and management processes. It aims to enhance efficiency, reduce processing time, and improve customer satisfaction by providing a robust platform for loan officers and customers.

Key Stakeholders / Actors

  • Loan Officers
  • Customers
  • IT Support
  • Compliance Officers

Functional Requirements

  • User authentication
  • Loan application submission
  • Loan approval workflow
  • Loan disbursement
  • Repayment tracking
  • Customer notifications

Non-Functional Requirements

  • High availability
  • Scalability
  • Security
  • Performance
  • Compliance with financial regulations
