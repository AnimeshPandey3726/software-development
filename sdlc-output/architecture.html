Architecture Overview

The LMS adopts a microservices architecture to ensure scalability, resilience, and independent deployability. Each business function is encapsulated within a separate service, communicating via REST APIs and message queues. This approach allows for rapid iteration and adaptation to changing business needs while maintaining a high level of system stability and security.
