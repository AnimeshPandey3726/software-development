Architecture Overview

The Phoenix Commerce Platform utilizes a microservices architecture to achieve scalability, resilience, and agility. Each business capability is implemented as an independent service, communicating through REST APIs and message queues. This approach allows for independent deployment and scaling of services, enabling rapid innovation and adaptation to changing business needs. Security is a top priority, with robust authentication, authorization, and data protection mechanisms in place. The platform is designed for high availability and performance, leveraging cloud-native technologies and best practices.
