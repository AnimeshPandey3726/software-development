Architecture Overview

The Login Management System utilizes a microservices architecture to provide a scalable and resilient authentication and authorization solution. Each service is independently deployable and scalable, allowing for flexibility and rapid iteration. Security is a primary concern, with encryption, RBAC, and comprehensive audit logging implemented throughout the system.
